Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2023

Introduction

Connect-a-Roo is committed to protecting your privacy, with special attention to children's privacy and safety. This application is designed for children with autism and their caregivers.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

  • Our application is designed for children with autism, including those under 13
  • We require verifiable parental consent
  • Parents/guardians must create and manage accounts
  • Parents can review and delete their child's information

Information We Collect

  • Parent/Guardian email address
  • Child's learning preferences and settings
  • User-generated content (managed by parents)
  • Basic device information
  • Essential usage data

How We Use Information

  • To provide educational services
  • To track learning progress
  • To customize learning experience
  • To ensure child safety
  • Never for marketing or advertising

Data Protection

  • Secure Firebase storage
  • Encrypted media files
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited data retention

Your Rights

Parents/guardians can:

  • Access their child's data
  • Correct information
  • Request data deletion
  • Export data
  • Revoke consent

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your parental rights, please contact us at support@connectaroo.com
